Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data this website collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

This website (Ichimoku Trend Screener) is operated as an independent personal project. If you have questions about this policy, you can reach out via the contact details associated with the screener.

What data we collect

This website is a static site and does not run a backend server. However, the following may occur:

  • Email address — if you voluntarily subscribe to updates via the email form on the homepage. This is handled by Buttondown, a third-party email service. See Buttondown's privacy policy for details.
  • Server logs — the hosting provider (Netlify) may collect standard web server logs including IP addresses and browser user-agent strings. See Netlify's privacy policy.
  • Cookies — this site does not set any first-party cookies. Third-party embeds (TradingView charts on stock detail pages) may set their own cookies. See TradingView's privacy policy.

Analytics

This site does not currently use Google Analytics or any other analytics platform. If analytics are added in future, this policy will be updated before they go live.

Advertising

This site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense or similar networks in the future. Ad networks may use cookies to serve personalised advertisements based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising via Google's Ad Settings. This policy will be updated if and when advertising is introduced.

Third-party links

This site contains links to external websites including TradingView and Buttondown. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies directly.

Your rights

If you have subscribed to email updates and wish to unsubscribe or request deletion of your email address, you can use the unsubscribe link in any email you receive, or contact us directly.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
