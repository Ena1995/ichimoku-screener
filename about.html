About This Screener

A free, rules-based tool for identifying stocks that meet defined trend and momentum conditions — updated daily, no sign-up required.

What it does

The Ichimoku Trend Screener scans UK and US listed equities each trading day and surfaces stocks where specific technical conditions are present: price above the Ichimoku cloud, a bullish cloud structure, and a minimum momentum score. It does not make predictions or give buy or sell signals. It simply shows which stocks currently meet the defined criteria.

What Ichimoku is

Ichimoku Kinko Hyo is a Japanese charting framework designed to assess trend direction, momentum, and support/resistance levels from a single view. The "cloud" (Kumo) is the most distinctive element — it represents a zone of potential support or resistance and gives a sense of trend strength and direction over time.

What this site is not

This is not a trading platform, broker, or financial adviser. The screener does not know your financial situation and cannot make recommendations suited to you personally. All output is educational and informational only.

Data & updates

Data is sourced from public market data feeds and updated automatically each trading day. The screener date shown on the dashboard reflects when the last run completed. There may be a short delay between market close and data availability.
